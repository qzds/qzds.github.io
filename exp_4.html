4. Product information decoding

We have achieved multi-level testing needs from daily real-time rapid testing to single-sample and multi-sample forensic-grade verification through various methods such as rapid kits, sample PCR, and precise sequencing.

（1）Kit assay
Experimental materials:
  • encapsulated samples
  • NaOH
  • HCl
  • basic nucleic acid amplification kits
  • chromogenic fluid
Experimental procedure：
  1. Treatment sample: Take a 10ul sample and add 10ulNaOH solution for high temperature treatment, add 10ulHCl for neutralization after 5min high temperature treatment, and then add 70ul water as a template for backup;
  2. Isothermal amplification: Prepare a thermostatic amplification system, activate it with 2ul of activator, and incubate it in an incubator at 37 °C for 20 min
  3. Sample color development: Take 3 μL of amplification product, dilute 1000 times, add 3.5 μL of color developer to 10 μL system, and let it stand at room temperature for 5 minutes to observe the color change.
（2）PCR test
Experimental materials:
  • Package samples
  • NaOH
  • HCl
  • Extaq Buffer
  • dNTP Mix
  • ExTaq
  • primers
  • Loading buffer
  • ddH₂O
  • Agarose gel
Experimental procedure：
  1. Sample treatment: Take a 10ul sample and add 10ulNaOH solution to high temperature treatment, add 10ulHCl for neutralization after 5min high temperature treatment, and then add 70ul water as a template for backup.
  2. PCR amplification: prepare a PCR amplification system and use a PCR instrument for amplification;
  3. Result verification: Add 2μL Loading buffer and perform DNA agarose gel electrophoresis to verify whether the band is correct.
（3）Precise sequencing

The processed samples are sent to a sequencing company for precise sequencing
