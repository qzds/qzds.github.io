Nature-Coded Assurance

Counterfeit products pose severe global economic and safety challenges, with existing authentication technologies such as QR codes and RFID tags often lacking sufficient security, durability, and accessibility. To address these issues, we present XMU-HeliCipher, an innovative anti-counterfeiting platform grounded in biomolecular design.

Our molecular pipeline makes product identity inseparable from the material itself—a fingerprint that cannot be forged, erased, or overlooked.

The Challenge We Solve

Counterfeit supply chains exploit gaps in current authentication tools. QR codes can be copied, RFID tags can be spoofed, and material tampering often goes undetected until it is too late. Authenticity should be as intrinsic as biology—which is why we turn to DNA.

Why DNA?

  • Billions of unique sequences from a microscopic footprint.
  • Chemically stable once encapsulated into coatings or substrates.
  • Readable by rapid assays or full sequencing for legal proof.
