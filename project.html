PROJECT-Description

Introduction

New-generation anti-counterfeiting technologies have become a market necessity

In the era of economic globalization, the volume and scope of commodity circulation are expanding day by day. Increasingly sophisticated counterfeiting techniques and a continuous stream of counterfeit and substandard products have emerged along with economic globalization. Counterfeit and substandard goods coexist with legitimate trade, undermining market integrity and causing significant losses to consumers and enterprises. Traditional anti-counterfeiting technologies, such as QR codes and RFID chips, are becoming increasingly easy to replicate and have serious limitations:

  1. Security: Barcodes and QR codes are structurally simple, making them easy to copy or to forge.
  2. Durability: Physical labels degrade due to wear, moisture or environmental stress.
  3. Accessibility: Many technologies (e.g., NFC/RFID) require specialized equipment and are vulnerable to hacking, which limits consumer verification.

Therefore, with the upgrading of consumption and enhancement of brand awareness, new-generation anti-counterfeiting technologies have become a market necessity.

XMU-HeliCipher：a DNA-based next-generation anticounterfeiting technique

Biological macromolecules, such as nucleic acids, possess an astonishing capacity for information storage. Research indicates that DNA stands out as the material with the highest information storage density, boasting a capacity up to six orders of magnitude. Given its microscopic size, DNA cannot be discerned with the naked eye or even a light microscope, rendering it highly concealed. More significantly, as a naturally occurring biomolecule, DNA exhibits excellent biocompatibility and is environmentally benign, posing minimal risk of adverse effects on living organisms or the ecosystem.
Of particular note is its potential for anti-counterfeiting measures: artificial DNA sequences, while compositionally similar to natural DNA, feature precise and intricate molecular structures. The arrangement of their bases is marked by a high degree of randomness and complexity, making accurate synthesis under normal conditions a formidable challenge. Specifically, by embedding DNA fragments carrying specific security information amidst a vast array of random or unrelated DNA sequences, the task of imitating or tampering with them becomes exceedingly difficult, thereby significantly elevating the bar for information forgery.

Based on these unique properties, we have developed the XMU-HeliCipher anti-counterfeiting system. Using our proprietary DNA encoding and decoding technology combined with synthetic biology approaches, we encapsulate synthetic DNA macromolecules into nanoparticles and integrate them into everyday materials—such as inks, packaging materials, or electronic chips—to assign each product a unique and unclonable identity. Furthermore, we have developed a rapid detection kit that enables real-time on-site verification of the anti-counterfeiting markers. This system ensures end-to-end traceability and authenticity throughout the entire product lifecycle, from manufacturing to distribution, effectively addressing counterfeiting issues. XMU-HeliCipher offers a forward-looking, cost-effective, and scalable solution, providing a practical and robust platform for next-generation anti-counterfeiting technology.

Objectives

Our project is anchored by four core goals:

  1. Unforgeable Encoding:
    Develop a proprietary algorithm to convert product identity information into unique DNA sequences, combining cryptographic security with ultra-high information density.
  2. Environmental Resilience:
    Engineer advanced encapsulation (e.g., silica coatings) to protect DNA markers from heat, oxidation, and UV radiation, ensuring long-term stability.
  3. Scalable Implementation:
    Optimize DNA synthesis and integration protocols for cost-efficiency, enabling adoption across industries—from luxury goods to pharmaceuticals.
  4. Accessible Verification:
    • Level 1: Portable nucleic acid amplification for field testing.
    • Level 2: Sequencing-based authentication for forensic or legal validation.

Impact

Our technology bridges synthetic biology and commerce to deliver:

  • Consumer Trust: Empowers buyers to verify authenticity effortlessly, protecting rights and safety.
  • Brand Integrity: Helps enterprises combat counterfeiting, reducing revenue loss and IP theft.
  • Regulatory Tools: Provides governments with forensic-grade evidence to enforce anti-fraud measures.

Future Vision

XMU-HeliCipher envisions DNA-based anti-counterfeiting as the global standard. Through pilot collaborations, toolkit expansion, and policy advocacy, we aim to transform markets into transparent, trustworthy ecosystems—turning the tide against counterfeiters one molecule at a time.
