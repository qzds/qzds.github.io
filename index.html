Addressing Global Challenges

Revolutionizing anti-counterfeiting with biomolecular design

The Problem

Counterfeit products pose severe global economic and safety challenges, with existing authentication technologies such as QR codes and RFID tags often lacking sufficient security, durability, and accessibility.

  • Easily replicated technologies
  • Limited security features
  • Poor durability
  • Complex verification processes

Our Solution

We present XMU-HeliCipher, an innovative anti-counterfeiting platform grounded in biomolecular design. We are redefining authenticity with nature's ultimate proof.

  • Unclonable DNA markers
  • Military-grade security
  • Superior stability
  • Multiple verification levels
